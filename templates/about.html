About Tripmakur

The smarter way to find your next destination.

Tripmakur is being built to help travelers discover trip ideas based on their budget, home city, interests, favorite past destinations, and travel style.

Free version now

This starter website uses a simple built-in destination matcher. It does not require paid APIs, user accounts, or a database.

Future version

Later, Tripmakur can add real-time flights, hotels, cruises, all-inclusive packages, restaurant ratings, activity suggestions, affiliate links, user accounts, and saved trip plans.
